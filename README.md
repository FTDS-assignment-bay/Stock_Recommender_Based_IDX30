# Introduction
In the dynamic world of stock investment, choosing the right company shares for investment with the individual investor profile is quite important in managing the risks that can occur. In this project, an exploration was carried out to develop Stock Investment Recommendations that were specifically designed based on the IDX30 Index List, where the companies listed in it have good fundamentals with different financial indicators, so it is necessary to group these companies so that they can be recommended according to the characteristics and preferences of investors. will invest.

# Backgrounds
Based on the results of a demographic survey conducted by KSEI, it is known that the number of investors in Indonesia as of February 2023 is around 10,623,731, with almost 60% of investors aged < 31 years and having an education level of high school. This indicates that some investors in Indonesia are still relatively young and their knowledge of the world of stock investment is still unfamiliar, so media or tools are needed that can provide an overview of the young investors' goals for investing and the potential risks they will experience when investing.

# Objective
Increase stock investment knowledge and insight for investors in general, and specifically for young investors, regarding their investment background & objectives and recommend shares from companies that have good fundamentals based on investor characteristics and preferences.
